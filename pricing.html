Frequently Asked Questions

What are the 14 security agents?+
OmniaGuard deploys 14 specialized AI security agents: Sentinel (perimeter), Cipher (encryption), Warden (access control), Specter (threat detection), Arbiter (policy enforcement), Nexus (API gateway), Phantom (stealth monitoring), Oracle (predictive analysis), Bastion (firewall), Aegis (data protection), Vigil (compliance), Prism (content filtering), Echo (audit logging), and Forge (key management).
How does the zero prompt injection guarantee work?+
OmniaGuard uses a multi-layered defense: input sanitization, semantic analysis, context isolation, and output validation. If any prompt injection bypasses our system, we provide a full incident report and remediation within 1 hour. Detection rate exceeds 99.97% across all known attack vectors.
Can I try before I buy?+
Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial with full access. No credit card required to start. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time.
What happens if I exceed my API call limit?+
We never cut off your security. If you approach your limit, we notify you and offer to upgrade. Overages billed at $0.001/call for Starter, $0.0005 for Professional. Enterprise has no limits.
Is OmniaGuard compatible with my existing AI stack?+
OmniaGuard integrates with all major AI frameworks: OpenAI, Anthropic, Google AI, Cohere, Hugging Face, and custom models. SDKs for Python, Node.js, Go, and REST API. Integration typically takes under 30 minutes.
How is cross-tenant isolation enforced?+
Each tenant operates in a cryptographically isolated namespace. Data, prompts, and model contexts are never shared. Hardware-level isolation on Enterprise, encrypted logical isolation on Professional.
