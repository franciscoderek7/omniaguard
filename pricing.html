Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade instantly, downgrade at end of billing cycle. No lock-in contracts for Personal and Professional tiers.

14-day full access to Professional tier features. No credit card required. Cancel anytime.

How does annual billing work?

Pay for 10 months, get 12. That's 2 months free — a 16.7% discount on any tier.

Is there a device limit for Enterprise?

No hard limit. Pricing is per 100 devices. Add devices as you grow — billing adjusts automatically.

What payment methods are accepted?

Stripe-powered: Visa, Mastercard, Amex, wire transfer for Enterprise/Empire. Crypto available on request.
