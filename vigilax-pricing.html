Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before deploying VIGILAX.

What makes VIGILAX different from other AI security products? +
VIGILAX is the only AI security system with a Constitutional Rights Gate built into the enforcement layer. Every proposed action — account lock, IP block, data freeze, legal filing — is reviewed by ARBITER, a specialized agent that applies Canadian Charter rights, PIPEDA, and PHIPA before any action executes. No other product does this.
Can I use VIGILAX for legal proceedings and regulatory complaints? +
Yes. The VAULT agent produces court-admissible evidence records with SHA-256 hash + RFC 3161 trusted timestamp + chain-of-custody log. VIGILAX Professional and Enterprise can generate formatted CCTS, CRTC, FCAC, and Ontario Small Claims filings automatically from sealed evidence records.
How does multi-tenant isolation work? +
Each tenant gets row-level security isolation in Supabase — your data is cryptographically separated from other tenants at the database layer. Your agents operate with your tenant context only. Cross-company threat intelligence is shared in anonymized, aggregated form only — never raw event data.
What happens to my data if I cancel? +
Evidence records under active legal proceedings are retained per your jurisdiction's requirements — they are never deleted unilaterally. All other data is exported to you in JSON format within 72 hours of cancellation, then permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days. You own your data.
Is VIGILAX patented? +
VIGILAX has five provisional patent claims pending, including the Constitutional Rights Gate (VIGILAX-IP-001), participant-recorded evidence sealing (VIGILAX-IP-002), and the three-tier LLM routing architecture (VIGILAX-IP-003). Enterprise customers receive access to the provisional patent documentation as part of white-glove onboarding.
What is the annual contract discount? +
All three tiers receive a 20% discount when billed annually. Basic: $949/year (vs $1,188). Professional: $4,790/year (vs $5,988). Enterprise: $23,990/year (vs $29,988). Annual contracts require a signed agreement and include an SLA upgrade.
